Methodology & Assumptions

LTVRevenue-based (no margin adjustment). Acct. Lifetime Spend from invoice data.
CACBlended: $111,186 total sales spend ÷ 32 working accounts. Applied uniformly across all cohorts.
CRO SalaryFull market-rate salary used (not reduced rate paid in period) to reflect current/future state.
Excluded from CACPartial CS team pre-sales time, COO/CFO involvement, salesops time. Noted as known exclusion for comparability.
ExclusionsFORIA ($0 revenue, ghosted beta) and 3 accounts with $750 first invoice (Chair King, Another Planet Entertainment, New West KnifeWorks) excluded — defunct pilot program. All 4 removed from both LTV and CAC denominator.
SlipstreamFirmographics manually entered: B2C, Sporting Goods, headcount ≤10, revenue unknown. Included in analysis.
ACV (CS Only)= Lifetime Spend (lifespan forced to 1 year per established methodology).
ACV (SaaS)= Avg. Spend/Year from invoice data.
SaaS + CSNo accounts in period. Baseline to be established on first close.
Data windowSept 2025 – Mar 2026. Pre-Sept data excluded due to inconsistent historical tracking.
